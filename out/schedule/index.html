BSIDES ahmedabad 2024

Event Timeline

12 & 13 October, 2024

The Forum, Club 07, Ahmedabad

Technical Talk

The technical talk section at BSides Ahmedabad is the place to be if you're passionate about hacking and cybersecurity. Here, you'll hear from the brightest minds in the industry, sharing their latest insights and research on the most cutting-edge topics. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, you'll find something to learn and be inspired by.

CXO & Women in security Track

The CXO & Women in security talk section at BSides ahmedabad is the perfect place to learn from the best and brightest in cybersecurity. Hear from CEOs, CTOs, and other senior leaders from leading cybersecurity companies and organizations as they share their insights on the latest trends, threats, and solutions.

Village Track

The village hacking section at bSides Ahmedabad is your chance to learn about car hacking and chip-off firsthand from experienced security professionals. See live demos of how to hack into cars and exploit security vulnerabilities in chips. You'll also have the opportunity to try your hand at these techniques yourself in a safe and controlled environment.

Trainings

Embark on a journey through cutting-edge cybersecurity domains with our comprehensive series. Discover the intricacies of Multi-Cloud Security, where you'll learn how to safeguard your digital ecosystem across various cloud environments. Take a Deep Dive into Fuzzing to uncover vulnerabilities and enhance software security, and explore the rapidly evolving landscape of Web3 Hacking as we navigate the next frontier of cybersecurity challenges. Finally, gain insights into the world of Web Application Hacking, where you'll unearth vulnerabilities in the digital age and learn how to protect your applications from threats.
